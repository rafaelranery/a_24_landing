Get to Know Us:

  • Where we started?
    A24 was founded on August 20, 2012, by film veterans Daniel Katz, David Fenkel, and John Hodges. Katz formerly led the film finance group at Guggenheim Partners, Fenkel was the president, co-founder and partner at Oscilloscope, and Hodges served as Head of Production and Development at Big Beach.
  • Where are we going?
    In 2023, A24 became the most nominated single studio of that year's ceremony with 18 total nominations between six of its films; Everything Everywhere All at Once (11 nominations, the most nominated film of that year's ceremony, including Best Picture), The Whale (3 nominations), and Aftersun, Causeway, Close and Marcel the Shell with Shoes On (each with 1 nomination).
  • Whats been going on?
    A24 earned its first Academy Award for Best Picture accolade for Moonlight in 2017 and again with Everything Everywhere All at Once in 2023, which became the studio's highest-grossing film; with the latter's numerous accolades, combined with one for another A24 film (The Whale), A24 became the first studio to win in all six major categories of the Academy Awards in a single year, as well as the most awarded film of all time.
